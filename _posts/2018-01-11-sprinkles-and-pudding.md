Sprinkles and Pudding... So who are Sprinkles and Pudding? Why they are green-cheeked conures of the genus Pyrrhura. They are quite compact and enjoy hanging about! Sprinkles is also known as Tillie and Pudding is known as Pudge. Maybe one day we will have a third upside-down bird of some description... Stay on this website for our fun adventures together! :)
