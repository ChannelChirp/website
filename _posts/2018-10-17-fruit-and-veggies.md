Fruit and vegetables are a good source of bird vitamins and bird minerals, including bird folate, bird vitamin C and bird potassium. They're an excellent source of bird dietary fibre, which can help to maintain a healthy bird gut and prevent bird constipation and other bird digestion problems. A diet high in bird fibre can also reduce your risk of bird bowel cancer.
