## What Green-cheeked Conures Eat

We love a varied diet of fresh fruits, vegetables, pellets, and seeds. Here's a typical menu:

* **Morning:** Apple slices, carrot sticks, and a sprinkle of high-quality pellets.
* **Afternoon:** Mixed leafy greens (kale, spinach) and a few sunflower seeds as a treat.
* **Evening:** A small portion of cooked brown rice or quinoa with chopped broccoli.

Always provide fresh water!
